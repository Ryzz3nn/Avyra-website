Server Rules

Follow these rules to ensure everyone has a great time

1

No RDM/VDM

Random Deathmatch and Vehicle Deathmatch are strictly prohibited. All kills must have valid RP reasoning.

2

Respect Everyone

Treat all players and staff with respect. Harassment, racism, and toxicity will result in immediate punishment.

3

Value Your Life

Act realistically in dangerous situations. If you're outnumbered or at gunpoint, comply and value your character's life.

4

No Metagaming

Don't use out-of-character information in roleplay. Keep IC (In Character) and OOC (Out of Character) separate.

5

Quality RP

Maintain high-quality roleplay at all times. Engage in meaningful interactions and stay in character.

6

No Combat Logging

Don't disconnect during active RP scenarios, especially during combat or police chases.
